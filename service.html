What is Agrivoltaics?

This revolutionary technology will be an integral part of our sustainable
future, so here's a quick explanation!

The Basics

Put simply, Agrivoltaics is the placement
of solar panels above crops.

The Science

The shade provided by solar panels cools
the air underneath. At the same time, plant
evapotranspiration increases ambient air
moisture, which also reduces temperature.

The result is a microclimate effect!

The Benefits

Under reduced heat stress, shade-tolerant
crops perform better.

Lower temperatures reduce evaporation,
which both improves soil quality and
reduces the amount of water needed to
yield the same crop growth.

Plants act as natural air conditoners
so solar can take advantage of sunlight
without getting too hot to perform
efficiently.
